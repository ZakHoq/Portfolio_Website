Favorite Book

Paradise Lost by John Milton

Paradise by John Milton is a classic of the western literary canon and a monumental work. It is an epic retelling of the book of genesis. it specifically is about Adam and Eve and their fall thanks to Satan. I enjoy the book heavily as it is a poem and I have loved poetry, and I adore the character writing. Satan, while still evil, is so charismatic and interesting that the story redefined cultueral depictions of the devil. It overall is fantastic, although hard for a newer audience to read due to its archaic langauge.
